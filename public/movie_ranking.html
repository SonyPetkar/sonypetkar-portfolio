The Best Web Series According To Sony Petkar

My Top 3 Web Series Of All-time.

1.Girl In The City

Acted by Mithila Palkar

The story is all about the girl shifting from her comfort zone i.e from her home town to a complete new place for her dreams.

2. Please Find Attached

Acted by Barkha Singh and Ayush

The story is about a couple who is in a living relationship and are in a same office. They both have their own way of looking towards their jobs and the office love story.

3. Adulting

Acted by Ayesha and Yashagini

The web series is all about the two friends living in different city from their home town, who have their different jobs, different goals but are stuck into the 9 to 5 cycle.
